\emph{The fish in the water is silent, the animal on the earth is noisy,} \\
\emph{the bird in the air is singing.} \\
\emph{But Man has in him the silence of the sea,} \\
\emph{the noise of the earth and the music of the air.} \\
--- Rabindranath Tagore

\vspace{2cm}

\emph{The will is not free—it is a prisoner of the species.} \\
\emph{The task of philosophy is to free it.} \\
--- Adapted from Schopenhauer

\end{flushright}
